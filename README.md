## 📖 The Idea

Most productivity applications treat planning as a checklist.

**The Athenaeum** treats it as a scholarly practice.

Inspired by:

* 📜 Antique parchment manuscripts
* ⌨️ Mechanical typewriters
* 🕯️ Dark academia aesthetics
* 📚 Historic libraries and archives
* 🎨 Dutch still-life paintings

The platform transforms goals, events, tasks, and deadlines into a living archive of personal achievement.

Every morning, your agenda is generated and "printed" onto parchment, creating an experience that feels less like task management and more like maintaining a personal chronicle.

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

The Athenaeum follows four principles:

### 1. Ritual Over Routine

Productivity should feel meaningful.

### 2. Calm Over Chaos

Minimal distractions and intentional design.

### 3. Archives Over Lists

Completed work becomes part of a permanent record.

### 4. Beauty With Function

Visual elegance should support productivity rather than compete with it.
